Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Taqwa ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account info: email address and display name (via Firebase Authentication).
  • Usage data: prayer habits, Quran bookmarks, challenge progress, quiz scores, and community posts — stored in Firestore to sync across devices.
  • Location: used only to calculate local prayer times. Your coordinates are never stored on our servers.
  • Device info: OS version and device model for crash diagnostics only.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve app features (prayer times, habit tracking, AI Scholar).
  • To sync your data across devices when you are signed in.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (authentication & database) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Anthropic Claude API (AI Scholar feature) — anthropic.com/privacy
  • Aladhan API (prayer times) — no personal data is sent

4. Data Retention

Your account data is kept as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen, which will permanently delete all associated data.

5. Children's Privacy

Taqwa is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
